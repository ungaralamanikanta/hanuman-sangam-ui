About Hanuman Sangam

Hanuman Sangam is a spiritual and community-based group formed to bring people together for devotion, unity, and service. जय बजरंगबली 🙏

🛕

Temple History

The temple is a sacred place where devotees gather to pray, seek blessings, and celebrate festivals with devotion and discipline since 2022.

🔱

Our Activities

  • Monthly Sangam meetings
  • Festival celebrations 🎉
  • Hanuman Jayanti puja
  • Community service drives
  • Food distribution (Prasad)
👥

Membership

12 dedicated members + 1 cashier managing all finances. Each member contributes monthly towards Sangam activities and temple maintenance.

🎯

Our Purpose

To maintain complete transparency, financial discipline, and unity among all members of the Hanuman Sangam family.

📅

Monthly Meeting

Held on the 5th of every month at 7:00 PM in the RAMALAYAM. All members are expected to attend.

💰

Financial Management

Monthly contributions tracked digitally. Full payment history available for each member. Admin-verified records updated in real time.
